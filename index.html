Problems I Solve Every Tax Season

These are the exact situations my clients come to me with

"I don't know which ITR form to file"

With multiple income sources — salary, capital gains, rental income — choosing the right ITR form is confusing. I ensure accurate filing with the correct form every time.

"My capital gains calculation seems off"

Whether it's equity, mutual funds, or property — capital gains rules are complex and change every budget. I apply the exact rules applicable to your transactions.

"GST compliance is taking too much of my time"

Deadlines, GSTR reconciliation, and ITC mismatches can be overwhelming. I handle your end-to-end GST compliance so you can focus on your business.

"I want to start a company but don't know where to begin"

From selecting the right structure (Pvt Ltd, LLP, OPC) to incorporation and post-registration compliance — I guide you through every step seamlessly.

"I received a notice from the Income Tax department"

Don't panic. Whether it's a mismatch notice or a scrutiny assessment, I handle the response professionally — protecting your interests at every stage.

"My books are a mess and audit is approaching"

Disorganized accounts can lead to costly errors. I clean up your books, ensure reconciliation, and get you audit-ready well before the deadline.
